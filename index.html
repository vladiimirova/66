About

We are an International Investment Group, the company has offices in New York, Geneva, and Singapore. Assuring discretion and confidentiality, our Group is specialized in wealth management, financial advice, tax planning as well as assistance in setting up all banking and financial operations. The family office strives to support only a limited number of people to offer very high-level services combining capital preservation, performance, and innovation. We see this as a testament to our ability to build lasting relationships with a demanding clientele.
